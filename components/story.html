Our Story

Authenticity, Hospitality, Connection

At The Sarabi Restaurant, our journey began with a simple passion: to bring people together over food that warms the heart and feeds the soul. Founded in Westlands, Nairobi, we blend family traditions with modern culinary art, crafting Pan-African fusion dishes from the freshest local ingredients. We pride ourselves on warm hospitality and a vibrant atmosphere, ensuring every visit feels like a memorable return home. Thank you for being part of our story.
